FAQ

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes. Plan changes take effect immediately. If you downgrade, you keep access to your current plan until the end of the billing period.

What happens if I exceed my rate limit?

Requests beyond your limit return a 429 Too Many Requests response. We never charge overage fees — upgrade to increase your limit.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

Yes — new accounts get a 14-day free trial of the Premium plan. No credit card required to start.

Do you offer discounts for startups or students?

Yes. Contact us via the support page and we'll work something out. We offer 50% off for verified students and early-stage startups.
